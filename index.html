Volumetric Video Challenges

on Compression and Sparse-View

SIGGRAPH Asia 2025 Workshop

Overview

The goal of this challenge is to accelerate the transition of volumetric video technology from laboratory prototypes to robust, production- and consumer-ready systems, enabling breakthroughs in immersive interactive experiences.

Real-time, free-viewpoint playback of dynamic 3D humans and scenes is foundational for a wide spectrum of AR/VR applications, including virtual filmmaking, remote collaborative telepresence, gaming, and digital twins.

This challenge addresses two core bottlenecks:

1. Compression

Given reconstruction quality metrics (e.g., PSNR, SSIM, perceptual similarity), minimize file size. This focuses on balancing storage efficiency and playback performance for practical deployment.

2. Sparse-View Reconstruction

Accurately reconstruct dynamic 3D humans from a minimal number of camera viewpoints (8 & 24 cameras), reducing hardware costs and simplifying capture setups.

Our challenge provides a unique opportunity for fostering cross-disciplinary discussion between computer vision, computer graphics, and AR/VR practitioners, uniting expertise in 3D reconstruction, generative AI, and immersive media systems.
